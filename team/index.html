üñº About the team

In 2018, She Can Tech was started with the mission to increase the meaningful participation of girls and women in science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) as an NCWIT AspireIT program.

Our team is made up of high school and university students who volunteer remotely year-long to bring opportunities to our communities.

We value:

  • Transparency We like open source anything.
  • Innovation Scrappy, determined, and adaptive.
  • Diversity You be you.
  • Empowerment Is there anything that I can do to support you?
  • Sustainability Build from the ground up.
